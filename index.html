FGO

Fate Grand Order focuses on people of historic figures with various legends, ranging from King Arthur to Shakespeare with their own twists/variations from their original stories.

Epic 7

Composed of original heroes consisting of many different designs, Epic Seven presents various skins for each character and their own unique battle style.

Girls Frontline

Girls Frontline is a game where anime girls are depicted as guns, named after the actual model of the gun and also arming themselves with the gun(s) themselves.

Arknights

A tower defense game where you deploy anime girls called Operators, who defend your position using the skills that all the different classes offer.

Genshin Impact

Genshin Impact centers around a open world RPG, giving a sense of adventure and action. Inspired by elements from Zelda: Breath of the Wild

Princess Connect!: Re-Dive

Based around characters from the Princess: Re-Dive series, Priconne is a side game that offers fast and efficient ways to get immersed in the gameplay
